Parallel Learning

Parallel learning encourages students to explore co-curricular activities alongside academics, fostering creativity, teamwork, and personal growth. From arts and sports to various skill-based activities, we ensure a well-rounded learning experience that prepares students for a dynamic future.

Journal Club

Journal Club helps students explore ideas share knowledge and enhance writing skills.

Guided Reading

Guided Reading helps students enhance comprehension build vocabulary and think critically.

Movie Time

Movie Time shows movies that provide useful information and help us learn new things.

Educational Tours

Educational Tours take students to places that help them learn more about history and science.

About Our School

In the year 2003, Mr. Katamreddy Sivakumar Reddy Garu, laid the foundation of “Gautami Vidya Dhamam School” with a conviction and philosophy to forge the right essence of education in the changing society while upholding a formidable moral conduct with impeccable attitude in the young minds.

The trails of GVD’s MISSION leads towards providing an outstanding mode of instruction that cultivates and prepares our students to forecast and confront real – life experiences and challenges with logical acumen and drive.
