About Our School

In the year 2003, Mr. Katamreddy Sivakumar Reddy Garu, laid the foundation of “Gautami Vidya Dhamam
School” with a conviction and philosophy to forge the right essence of education in the changing society
while upholding a formidable moral conduct with impeccable attitude in the young minds.

We weaved our VISION to function as a robust organization in providing comprehensive education which
focuses on holistic learning, innovative pedagogies, and help nourish and develop the scholastic and
co-scholastic abilities of our students.

The trails of GVD’s MISSION leads towards providing an outstanding mode of instruction that cultivates
and prepares our students to forecast and confront real – life experiences and challenges with logical
acumen and drive.

Gautami Vidya Dhamam School’s approach to bring life to its Conviction, Vision and Mission all come under one canopy.
